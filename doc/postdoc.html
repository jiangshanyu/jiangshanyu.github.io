Open postdoc positions

Monash Blockchain Technology Centre (MBTC) at Monash University, Melbourne, Australia, is looking for two postdoctoral on the topic of blockchain. The salary is 92,792 -- 120,093 per year (Australian dollars) plus 17% Superannuation.

Institution

Monash is one of the prestigious "Group of Eight" universities in Australia. It is ranked #55 in the QS World University Rankings (2021) and #64 by Times Higher Education World University Rankings (2021). The postdoc will be part of the Monash Blockchain Technology Centre (MBTC) at Monash University, working with Dr. Jiangshan Yu (Associate Director of Research, MBTC). MBTC has strong collaboration between academics, industries and governments, both locally and internationally.

The position

Eligibility: Candidates must meet the following criteria.

  • Ph.D. in Computer Science or Mathematics
Desired skills: Evidence of knowledge and research experience in at least one of the following topics would be beneficial:
  • Cryptography
  • Blockchain
  • Dependability and fault tolerance
  • Distributed and parallel computing

Deadline

There is no deadline. The position is on a first come, first served basis.
