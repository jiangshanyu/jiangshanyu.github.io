    Short Biography

    Jiangshan Yu is an EPSRC project funded Ph.D student working with Prof. Mark Ryan on the project " Analysing Security and Privacy Properties" in the School of Computer Science, University of Birmingham, U.K. He received his MSc and MPhil degree in computer science (information security, under the supervision of Dr. Guilin Wang and Prof. Yi Mu) respectively in 2011 and 2012, from the University of Wollongong, Australia. His research interests are in security and privacy. In particular, the focus of his research has been on secure authentication, PKI, key management, certificate security, and email security. He is also interested in cloud security and e-voting security.
